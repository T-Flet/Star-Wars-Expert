This unnamed woman served as an Imperial controller aboard a Star Destroyer during the Battle of Hoth. She communicated with Zuckuss, a Gand bounty hunter, concerning the ninety Rebels that he was rescuing from a downed transport. The woman informed him that the information would be relayed to Imperial command.
