The title Captain Antilles was held, at some point, by more than one person:

Canon
Raymus Antilles, was the captain of Tantive IV from the time of the Clone Wars until his death in 0 BBY.
Wedge Antilles, who was not related to Raymus, held the rank of captain in the New Republic Defense Fleet during the last stages of the Galactic Civil War.
Legends
Raymus Antilles, referred to in some sources as Colton Antilles, was the captain of Tantive IV from the time of the Clone Wars until his death in 0 BBY
Wedge Antilles, who was not related to Raymus, was a captain in the Rebel Alliance from 4 ABY to 6.5 ABY
