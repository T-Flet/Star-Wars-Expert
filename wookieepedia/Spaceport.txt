Spaceports, also known as starports, were facilities where starships and other spacecraft would dock and take off from. Mos Eisley was a spaceport settlement on the desert planet of Tatooine. Starports were grouped in three classes: stellar, standard, and limited services. Baggage-robos were a type of droid that helped passengers with bulky luggage and were often found at starports.
