A mysterious humanoid female was a prisoner of the Nihil aboard the Korvix Vorn prison ship. In captivity, the woman wore a mask and tan prison jumpsuit. The humanoid was of a species with pointed ears. The woman also had light skin and black hair tied back in a hairbun. One day, the woman and numerous other prisoners were brought to an open area to test which of them was a Jedi in hiding.
