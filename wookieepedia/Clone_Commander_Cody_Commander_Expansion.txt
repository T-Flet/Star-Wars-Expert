The Clone Commander Cody Commander Expansion is a commanding officer expansion to the Star Wars: Legion infantry game by Atomic Mass Games. The expansion adds Clone Commander CC-2224 "Cody" to the Galactic Republic faction and came out on April 21, 2023.
