
This article is about the caste. You may be looking for Gallius Rax, who used the codename the Operator when feeding information to the New Republic.

"Gree units fulfill four caste-assignments: Crafter, Operator, Researcher and Administrator. I am Operator. I ensure proper function of Gree ingenuity."
―Hib-R-ak
The Operator caste of the Gree were responsible for the operation and manipulation of devices of Gree technology.
