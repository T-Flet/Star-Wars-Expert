Commander was the rank or title of an officer with authority over a body of troops or a military operation. In galactic history, soldiers served under commanders in various military organizations such as the Republic Military, which used Clone Commanders such as Cody and Jedi Commanders during the Clone Wars. on the battlefield. The Confederacy military had General Grievous overseeing the Separatist Droid Army as Supreme Martial Commander during the Clone Wars. Similarly, the Imperial Army, the Alliance to Restore the Republic, the New Republic, the First Order military, and the Resistance all had commanders within their ranks. In the case of the First Order military, Commanders also were identified by wearing teal uniforms similar to that of Majors and Colonels. During the Clone Wars, Mitth'raw'nuruodo claimed he served as a commander in the Chiss Expansionary Defense Fleet of the Chiss Ascendancy despite actually being a Senior Captain.
