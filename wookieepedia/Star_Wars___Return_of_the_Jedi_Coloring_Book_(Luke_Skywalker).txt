The Star Wars: Return of the Jedi Coloring Book was published by Kenner Products in 1983 and features illustrations by Susan Nelson. It shares the same title with two other Kenner coloring books, another published in 1983 and featuring Max Rebo on the cover, and one published in 1984 featuring Lando Calrissian. This particular coloring book features Luke Skywalker on the cover and is identified by the product number 18420.

Continuity
The book uses some alternate names for characters, such as Droopy McCool being named "Der Droopy", and Wam Lufba only referred to as Yuzzum, his species' name. Most characters seen are not identified at all.
