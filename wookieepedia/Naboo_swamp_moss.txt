Naboo swamp moss was a type of moss native to the planet Naboo. The moss grew in sheets and hung from the branches of trees on the borders of Naboo's swamps.

Behind the scenes
Naboo swamp moss was briefly mentioned in MedStar I: Battle Surgeons, a 2004 Clone Wars novel written by Steve Perry and Michael Reaves as the first part of the MedStar Duology.
