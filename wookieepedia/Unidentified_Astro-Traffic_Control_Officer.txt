A human officer lived during the Imperial Era and worked for Preox-Morlana as an Astro-Traffic Control Officer in 5 BBY. When Deputy Inspector Syril Karn investigated about the Kenari human male murdered two officers, he found out where his ship went after he left Morlana One.
