Naboo Fighter Pilots were pilots who served in the Royal House of Naboo's Space Fighter Corps. Even though she wasn't a member of the Space Fighter Corps, Queen Sosha Soruna earned a fighter pilot certification a year before her election as Queen of Naboo.
