    
     Lieutenant Pol Treidum was a gantry officer.  
Gantry officer was an officer role used within the Galactic Empire. In 0 BBY, Lieutenant Pol Treidum was a gantry officer on board the Death Star superweapon, where he worked in the command office that overlooked Docking Bay 327 with his aide.
