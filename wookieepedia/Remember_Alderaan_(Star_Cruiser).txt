The Remember Alderaan was a Mon Calamari Star Cruiser used by the New Republic.

History
The battle cruiser was said to be a new ship at the time of the Battle of Mindor, and was under the command of Lando Calrissian. It arrived later to the battle over Mindor than the main Rapid Response Task Force sent by the New Republic.
