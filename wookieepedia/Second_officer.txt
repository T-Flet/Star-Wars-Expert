Second officer was a position held by an individual who served as third in command of a starship, behind the Executive officer. Toshma Jefkin served as the second officer aboard the Tantive IV. Plikh'ar'illmorf served as Second officer aboard the Chiss Expansionary Defense Fleet warship Springhawk.
