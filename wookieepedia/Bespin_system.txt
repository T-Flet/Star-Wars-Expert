The Bespin system was a star system situated in the Anoat sector in the Outer Rim Territories. Miser, Orin, Velser's Ring and Bespin were part of the system. Located in the clouds of Bespin, Cloud City was a small Tibanna gas mining colony that tried to avoid Imperial detection, but was unable to do so following the Battle of Hoth.
