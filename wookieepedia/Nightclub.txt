―Doctor Aphra, to Just Lucky
     Zoulhaus, a nightclub on Galathor III  
Nightclubs were establishments found throughout the galaxy. Axel Greylark, the son of Galactic Republic Supreme Chancellor Kyong Greylark was bailed out from nightclubs on numerous instances by his mother's assistant. One nightclub was Zoulshaus, found on Galator III.
