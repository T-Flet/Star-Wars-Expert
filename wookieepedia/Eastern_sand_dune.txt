The eastern sand dune was located on the desert planet Serolonis. Between 19 BBY and 18 BBY, the droid racer TAY-0 heard a rumor that the last Riot Racing sponsor who had failed to pay the gangster Grini Millegi took a "permanent pit stop" in the dune.
