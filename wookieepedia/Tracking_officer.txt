Tracking officer, or tracking systems specialist was a position within the starfleet of the Galactic Empire. His or her duty was to track enemy starships. In Star Destroyers, such officers worked in the data pits located on either sides of the bridge's platform.
In 3 ABY, Commander Nemet served as tracking officer aboard the Avenger under Captain Lorth Needa.
