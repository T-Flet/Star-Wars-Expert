Lure of the Lost is a downloadable adventure for Fantasy Flight Games' Star Wars: Force and Destiny roleplaying game. It was released on June 18, 2015, in conjunction with the Star Wars: Force and Destiny Beginner Game and serves as a direct followup to the adventure Mountaintop Rescue.
