Red Seven was the callsign used by the seventh member of Red Squadron.

History
During the Galactic Civil War, Cadet Harb Binli flew as Red Seven during the Battle of Scarif, and at the Battle of Yavin.
Keir Santage flew as Red Seven at the Battle of Endor.[source?]
During the hijacking of the Harbinger, another rebel pilot flew as Red Seven.
